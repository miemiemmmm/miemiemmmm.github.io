I am a PhD student in the Department of Biochemistry at the University of Zurich. I am proactively exploring the interface between Molecular dynamics simulation, Machine Learning to accelerate drug discovery. I am particularly interested in the development of a computational framework to facilitate the machine learning understanding of the molecular interaction pattern using 3D-based geometric deep learning.
